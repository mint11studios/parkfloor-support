# Park Floor - Support
                                                                                                                                                                                        
  ## About
                                                                                                                                                                                        
  Park Floor automatically detects when you arrive at your parking garage and lets you record your floor with one tap. Check your parked floor anytime from the home screen widget or   
  Apple Watch.

  ## Common Questions                                                                                                                                                                   
                                                        
  **How does parking detection work?**                                                                                                                                                  
  Park Floor uses your garage's location to detect when you arrive. When parking is detected, you'll get a notification to select your floor.
                                                                                                                                                                                        
  **Does the app track my location?**                   
  No. Park Floor only monitors a geofence around your saved garage location. No location data is stored, shared, or sent anywhere. Everything stays on your device.                     
                                                                                                                                                                                        
  **The widget isn't updating**
  Try removing and re-adding the widget. Make sure the app has "Always" location permission in Settings > Park Floor > Location.                                                        
                                                                                                                                                                                        
  **How do I change my garage location?**
  Open the app > Setup > Update Location. Make sure you're at your garage when you do this.
