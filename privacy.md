  ## Overview                                                                                                                                                                           
   
  Park Floor operates entirely on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data.                                                                                 
                                                        
  ## Data We Do NOT Collect                                                                                                                                                             
   
  - Location data                                                                                                                                                                       
  - Usage analytics                                     
  - Personal information
  - Device identifiers
  - Crash reports
                                                                                                                                                                                        
  ## On-Device Data
                                                                                                                                                                                        
  The app stores the following data locally on your device only:                                                                                                                        
   
  - Your garage location (coordinates you provide)                                                                                                                                      
  - Floor configuration and names                       
  - Calibration data                                                                                                                                                                    
  - Last parking event                                  
                                                                                                                                                                                        
  This data is stored in your device's local storage and is never transmitted off your device. It is shared only between the main app and the home screen widget using Apple's App Group
   feature.                                                                                                                                                                             
                                                                                                                                                                                        
  ## Permissions                                        

  - **Location:** Used solely to detect when you arrive at your saved garage. Not tracked, logged, or shared.                                                                           
  - **Motion Activity:** Used to detect driving-to-walking transitions for automatic parking detection. Not stored or shared.
